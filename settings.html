BunMoji Sidecar-driven sprite expressions
v0.1
A sidecar LLM picks character expressions and scene backgrounds before the main model generates. No more guessing emotions from text — the AI chooses intentionally.
Enables sidecar-driven expressions and suppresses ST's built-in classifier.

Override sprites that activate when narrative conditions are met. Evaluated by sidecar before each generation.
